Why Segregate Waste?

Proper waste segregation is the first step towards recycling and a circular economy. Here’s why it matters.

🌍

Reduces Landfill Waste

Segregating waste ensures that recyclable materials don't end up in landfills, reducing pollution and land usage.

♻

Promotes Recycling

Clean, segregated waste is easier and cheaper to recycle. Mixed waste often becomes contaminated and unusable.

⚡

Conserves Resources

Recycling paper, glass, and plastic saves raw materials and energy compared to producing everything from scratch.
